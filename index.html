FAQ

What key switches are available for the Keychron V1?

The Keychron V1 offers Gateron mechanical switches in three options: tactile, linear, and clicky.

Is the keyboard compatible with Mac and Windows?

Yes, it is fully compatible with both macOS and Windows, with dedicated keys for both systems.

How do I customize the keyboard's backlighting?

Our intuitive software allows you to adjust the backlighting, including effects and brightness, for a personalized experience.

What is the warranty on the Keychron V1?

The Keychron V1 comes with a 1-year warranty for any manufacturing defects.
